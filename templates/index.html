Knot Puzzle Activity Instructions

  1. Look closely at the knot diagram.

    Pay special attention to the crossings ‚Äî which strand goes over and which goes under.

    • You can enlarge the image if needed by clicking the image then clicking the x to return to the game board.
  2. Drag rope pieces from the piece bank onto the board to begin building.
  3. Rotate pieces with the arrow keys until they line up with the diagram.
  4. Adjust crossing order by right-clicking:
    • Right-click to move a piece above another.
    • Right-click again to move it below if needed.
  5. Fix any mistakes with the trash can.
    • Drag extra pieces into the trash can and confirm to delete it.
    • Double Click the trash can to clear the playing board.
  6. Match the crossings, not the overall aesthetic.

    The exact placement or curve doesn't matter as much as getting the over/under crossings correct.

  7. Finish and move on.

    Once your knot matches the diagram, continue to the next one. There are seven knots in total.

If you have any questions during the activity, please feel free to ask.
